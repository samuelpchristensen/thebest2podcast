About the Podcast

Welcome to "The Best 2 Podcast" — an upbeat podcast that explores the secular benefits of LDS missions, emphasizing practical lessons and valuable advice. Join accomplished individuals from The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints as they share how missions catalyze personal, professional, and vocational triumphs, revealing profound impacts on careers, relationships, and life's purpose.
